Why Ceramic Coating?

AN INVESTMENT THAT LASTS

With a coating that’s made to last, your vehicle’s exterior and interior will be protected for a lifetime. Not only will your car’s condition remain impressive, but its resale value will be substantially higher.

SLICK NEW LOOK

A look this good only comes with a world class coat that speaks for itself. Bring out your vehicle’s colors and top it off with timeless glossy finish for good.

LESS MAINTENANCE, MORE SHINE

We double dare dirt, brake dust and tar to stick to your paint, wheels or glass. Your slick new surface acts as a non-stick shield to make washing easier, faster and less frequent. KENZO made waxing obsolete.

EXPERIENCE THE WORLDS FIRST 10H CERAMIC COATING. NOTHING COMES CLOSE TO COMPARISON

KENZO glass coating has a minimum hardness of 10H – while the average rests between 2H and 4H. This means you’re getting the hardest glass coating on the entire market to protect your vehicle and its original paint like never before. If scratches do dare to leave a mark, it’ll come off easy with light polishing which won’t do any damage to the coat itself. Since we’re talking a 10H level, you can expect your paint to remain timeless and in mint condition due to the thick UV protection layer.

With nanotechnology in the works, you can bet every pore will be filled to give you that glossy, slick, and self-cleaning finish. It’ll be a real challenge for contaminants like dirt, tar, bird-droppings and even graffitti to hold on and stick to a 10H surface. Especially when it rains, water will curl and roll like beads – hence the self-cleaning effect. Less grime. Less hassle. A one-time permanent application and solution. It’s your call.
